ELEONORA PARIS
Architetto Dipl. USI/AAM
e@eleonoraparis.com
Eleonora Paris, born in Varese (IT) in 1994, graduated in architecture at the Accademia di Architettura di Mendrisio in 2019. She worked in the office of Valerio Olgiati from 2021 to 2026, leading mainly international projects, and worked as a teaching assistant in the Accademia di Architettura di Mendrisio from 2020 to 2021. In 2026, she founded her own practice, based between Switzerland and Italy.
